Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026. This policy describes the CleanLiving mobile app (“the App”). It is provided for transparency and is not legal advice.

What we collect

  • Camera image (label scan): When you scan a product label, the App captures an image so it can read ingredients and produce a result.
  • Scan results and history (on your device): The App stores scan results locally to power features like your audit log and aggregate scores shown in the App.

How we use data

  • To analyze labels: The scan image is used to generate ingredient lists, highlights, notes, scores, and suggested alternatives shown in the App.
  • To show your progress: On-device history is used to display past scans and related summaries in the App.

Where data is stored

  • On-device: Scan history is stored on your device (for example using local database storage).
  • When you use cloud analysis: If the App is configured to use a secure HTTPS endpoint you or the developer operate, the scan image may be sent to that service to perform analysis via a model provider, and a structured result is returned to the App. That service should be configured to minimize retention consistent with its own policies.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. If analysis is performed through a third-party model provider, images or derived content may be processed by that provider as part of generating results, according to the provider’s terms and policies.

Affiliate links

The App may show affiliate links. If you purchase through an affiliate link, CleanLiving may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
