CleanLiving

Scan labels. Spot toxic ingredients. Swap instantly.

CleanLiving is an iOS app that helps you quickly understand what’s in the products you use every day by scanning ingredient labels and highlighting ingredients you may want to avoid.

Join the Waitlist

How it works

  1. Scan an ingredient label using the camera
  2. See highlights for ingredients flagged as “caution” or “avoid”
  3. Get a Purity Score (1–100) to compare products fast
  4. Tap one time to view a cleaner alternative

What you’ll see

  • Live Vision Scanner: a guided overlay to align labels for clearer scans
  • Toxic Hit Highlighting: flagged ingredients highlighted in color
  • Hormone Impact Notes: when relevant, learn how certain chemicals may affect the body
  • Microplastic Warnings: quick flags for synthetic materials and plastic exposure concerns
  • My Home Audit Log: past scans saved so you can track progress over time
